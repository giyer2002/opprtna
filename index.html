About Opprtna Therapeutics

Opprtna Therapeutics is a biotechnology company focused on developing aptamer-based therapies for a wide range of diseases. Our mission is to leverage the power of aptamers to deliver novel, safe, and effective treatments to patients.

Aptamers are synthetic single-stranded DNA or RNA molecules that bind to specific targets with high affinity and selectivity. This makes them ideal candidates for developing targeted therapies that can modulate disease pathways with minimal side effects.

Our team of experienced scientists is committed to pushing the boundaries of aptamer technology to develop innovative therapies that address unmet medical needs.

Our Technology

Opprtna Therapeutics has developed a proprietary platform for discovering, engineering, and manufacturing high-quality aptamers. Our technology platform enables us to:

  • Identify and select aptamers with optimal binding affinity and selectivity for specific targets.
  • Engineer aptamers with desired properties, such as enhanced stability, pharmacokinetic profile, and therapeutic efficacy.
  • Develop scalable and cost-effective manufacturing processes for aptamer-based therapeutics.

Pipeline

Opprtna Therapeutics has a robust pipeline of aptamer-based therapeutics in various stages of development. Our current focus areas include:

  • Oncology: Targeting cancer cells and signaling pathways.
  • Infectious Diseases: Developing aptamers against viral and bacterial pathogens.
  • Autoimmune Diseases: Modulating immune responses to treat autoimmune disorders.

We are actively seeking partners to collaborate on the development and commercialization of our aptamer-based therapies.
